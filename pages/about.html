Our Story

🍷 Est. 2009

Passion Poured Into Every Glass

Boutique Wine Events was born from a simple belief: that wine is best experienced as a shared journey. Founded in 2009 by master sommelier Elena Marchetti and her husband, vintner Carlo Marchetti, we have been crafting intimate, world-class wine events that educate, inspire, and delight.

Over 15 years, we've hosted more than 240 events across 38 wine regions — from sun-drenched Napa valleys to candlelit Parisian cellars. Every event reflects our core philosophy: that exceptional wine is a story waiting to be told.

Values That Guide Us

More than just a business, we are a community of enthusiasts dedicated to the art of the vine.

Integrity & Authenticity

We partner directly with family-owned vineyards and independent vintners to ensure every bottle we present has a traceable, honest heritage.

Lifelong Education

Wine is a language. We believe in empowering our guests with knowledge, from understanding soil chemistry to the nuances of glass shapes.

Environmental Stewardship

We prioritise organic and biodynamic estates that treat the land with the respect it deserves, ensuring world-class wine for generations to come.
